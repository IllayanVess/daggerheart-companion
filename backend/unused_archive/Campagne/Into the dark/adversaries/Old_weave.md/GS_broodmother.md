## GIANT SPIDER BROODMOTHER

Tier 2 Leader

A nightmare given flesh—a spider the size of a wagon, her abdomen swollen with eggs, her eight eyes gleaming with ancient cunning. The ruin is her cathedral, and you are the sacrifice.

Motives & Tactics: Protect the egg-sac at all costs, command her children, corner prey, consume the strong first

Difficulty: 17 | Thresholds: 14/28 | HP: 10 | Stress: 6
ATK: +2 | Fangs: Melee | 2d10+6 phy

Experience: Brood Commander +3, Terrifying Presence +2

# FEATURES

Mother's Mandate – Passive: All allied Giant Spider adversaries within Close range gain +1 to their attack rolls while the Broodmother is conscious.

Summon Eggguard – Action: Spend a Fear to summon 1d4 Giant Spider Eggguards (see below). They appear at Close range of the Broodmother and immediately take the spotlight in any order you choose.

Poison Oviposit – Action: Make an attack against a Restrained or Prone target within Melee range. On a success, deal 2d8 direct physical damage and the target must mark 2 Stress. If this reduces the target to 0 HP, describe how the Broodmother injects eggs into the dying body.

Desperate Screech – Reaction: When the Broodmother marks half or more of her HP (5+ damage taken), you can spend 1 Fear to force all PCs within Close range to make a Presence Reaction Roll (17) . On a failure, they become Frightened (disadvantage on attacks against spiders) until the end of the scene.

Thrash – Reaction: When the Broodmother takes damage from a creature within Melee range, you can mark 1 Stress to make a standard attack against that creature.

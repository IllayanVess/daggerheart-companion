## GIANT SPIDER WEBSPINNER

Tier 2 Ranged

A bulbous spider the size of a small cart, perched high in the rafters. It rarely descends, preferring to wrap prey from a distance in sticky silk before approaching.

Motives & Tactics: Maintain distance, immobilize from above, protect egg-sacs, flee to higher ground

Difficulty: 15 | Thresholds: 10/20 | HP: 6 | Stress: 5
ATK: +3 | Silk Spit: Far | 2d8+3 phy

Experience: Weaver +2, Elevated Positioning +3

# FEATURES

Rafter Perch – Passive: The Webspinner begins combat at Far range vertically (on ceiling or high wall). Melee attacks against them have disadvantage unless the attacker can reach their elevation.

Web Bolas – Action: Make an attack against a target within Far range. On a success, deal 2d6 physical damage and the target is Restrained (escape DC 13, action to attempt). A restrained target cannot move further from the Webspinner until they escape.

Web Spray – Action: Spend a Fear to target a group within Close range of a point you choose. All targets in that area must make an Agility Reaction Roll (15) . On a failure, they become Vulnerable and Slowed (half movement) until they spend an action to tear away the clinging silk.

Tether Pull – Reaction: When a creature Restrained by the Webspinner attempts to escape and fails, you can mark 1 Stress to pull them one range band closer to the Webspinner (e.g., Far → Close, Close → Melee).

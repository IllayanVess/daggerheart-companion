## GIANT SPIDER STALKER

Tier 1 Skulk

A long-limbed spider the size of a hunting hound, its mottled grey carapace nearly invisible against webbed stone. It waits motionless for hours, then strikes without warning.

Motives & Tactics: Ambush from above, isolate prey, retreat when wounded, use webs to control movement

Difficulty: 12 | Thresholds: 5/10 | HP: 4 | Stress: 3
ATK: +2 | Bite: Melee | 1d8+2 phy

Experience: Ambush +3, Climb +2

# FEATURES

Lurker – Passive: The Stalker begins combat Hidden. While Hidden, their first attack has advantage. They become visible after attacking.

Web Trap – Action: Choose a target within Close range. The target must make an Agility Reaction Roll (12) . On a failure, they become Restrained (escape DC 11, action to attempt). On a success, they mark 1 Stress.

Pounce – Action: Spend a Fear to make a standard attack with advantage against a target within Very Close range. On a success, deal an additional 1d6 physical damage and the target is knocked Prone.

Skittering Retreat – Reaction: When the Stalker marks 2 or more HP from a single attack, you can mark 1 Stress to move them up to Close range away from the attacker and become Hidden (as their Cloaked action).

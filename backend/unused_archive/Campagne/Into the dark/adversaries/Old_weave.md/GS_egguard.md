## GIANT SPIDER EGGGUARD

Tier 1 Minion

A pale, translucent spider the size of a house cat, its body distended with undeveloped eggs. It throws itself at threats with suicidal fury, buying time for the Broodmother to escape or for other eggs to hatch.

Motives & Tactics: Die for the brood, overwhelm with numbers, latch onto prey, buy time

Difficulty: 11 | Thresholds: None | HP: 1 | Stress: 1
ATK: -2 | Bite: Melee | 1 phy

Experience: Swarm Tactics +2

# FEATURES

Minion (4) – Passive: The Eggguard is defeated when they take any damage. For every 4 damage a PC deals to the Eggguard, defeat an additional Minion within range the attack would succeed against.

Group Attack – Action: Spend a Fear to choose a target and spotlight all Giant Spider Eggguards within Close range of them. Those Minions move into Melee range of the target and make one shared attack roll. On a success, they deal 1 physical damage each. Combine this damage.

Explosive Rupture – Reaction: When an Eggguard is defeated within Melee range of a creature, you can mark its 1 Stress to deal 1d4 direct physical damage to the nearest creature as its egg-sac bursts in a spray of caustic fluid. This damage cannot be reduced by armor.

## FAERIE GUARD

Tier 2 Standard

A tall, silent faerie in woven thorn-armor, carrying a spear of hardened root and a shield of polished bark. They stand at the village gates and do not smile.

Motives & Tactics: Hold the line, protect civilians, die before retreating, obey the Elder without question

Difficulty: 15 | Thresholds: 12/22 | HP: 7 | Stress: 4
ATK: +2 | Root Spear: Melee | 2d8+5 phy

Experience: Guardian's Vigil +3, Formation Fighting +2

# FEATURES

Shield of Bark – Passive: The Guard has +2 Difficulty against ranged attacks (effective Difficulty 17) as their bark shield turns arrows and bolts.

Spear Wall – Action: Mark 1 Stress to make a standard attack against all targets within Melee range. Roll once and compare to each target's Difficulty. On a success, deal 2d6+4 physical damage to each target hit.

Protect the Tender – Reaction: When a Faerie Root Tender within Very Close range of the Guard is targeted by an attack, the Guard may swap positions with that Root Tender and become the new target of the attack. The Guard then takes half damage from that attack (rounded up) as their bark shield absorbs the blow.

Root-Bound Resolve – Passive: The Guard cannot be Frightened or Charmed while within Close range of the Faerie Elder or the village gates.

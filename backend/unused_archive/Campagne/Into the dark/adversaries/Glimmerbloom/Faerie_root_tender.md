## FAERIE ROOT TENDER

Tier 2 Minion

A small, moss-bearded fey with bark-like skin and gentle hands, tending to the great roots with whispered songs. They rarely fight, but when the village is threatened, they rise with surprising fury.

Motives & Tactics: Protect the roots, flee from real danger, call for help, use terrain to escape

Difficulty: 12 | Thresholds: None | HP: 1 | Stress: 1
ATK: +1 | Root-Cutter: Melee | 4 phy

Experience: Rootcraft +2, Hide in Foliage +2

# FEATURES

Minion (5) – Passive: The Root Tender is defeated when they take any damage. For every 5 damage a PC deals to the Root Tender, defeat an additional Minion within range the attack would succeed against.

Group Attack – Action: Spend a Fear to choose a target and spotlight all Faerie Root Tenders within Close range of them. Those Minions move into Melee range of the target and make one shared attack roll. On a success, they deal 4 physical damage each. Combine this damage.

Root Hide – Reaction: When a Root Tender is targeted by an attack, you can mark their 1 Stress to have them duck behind a root or into a hollow. The attack has disadvantage. If it still succeeds, the Root Tender is defeated as normal.
